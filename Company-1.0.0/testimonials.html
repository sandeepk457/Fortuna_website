Industries We Empower

we understand that every industry has unique challenges, but one common need: a resilient, intelligent, and future-ready supply chain. Our solutions are designed to bring automation, visibility, and performance improvements across every stage of the supply chain life cycle.

Manufacturing

We help manufacturers streamline complex supplier networks, optimize production schedules, and implement just-in-time processes. With Fortuna’s automated solutions, manufacturers gain real-time visibility into inventory, reduce downtime, and ensure consistent product quality.

Retail & E-Commerce

In a fast-moving retail world, speed and efficiency are critical. Our platforms enable automated order management, demand forecasting, and last-mile delivery optimization—ensuring customers receive the right products, at the right time, every time.

Automotive

Fortuna supports the automotive sector with advanced logistics planning, vendor coordination, and predictive maintenance scheduling. From global parts movement to plant-level production support, our systems reduce lead times and enhance supply chain agility.

Pharmaceuticals & Healthcare

Medical safety and regulatory compliance are non-negotiable. We provide automated solutions for cold chain logistics, product traceability, and compliance monitoring—ensuring life-saving medicines and healthcare products reach destinations safely and efficiently.

Energy & Utilities

Managing critical assets and global suppliers requires precision. Fortuna enables smart procurement, real-time monitoring, and risk management systems that ensure uninterrupted operations and compliance with sustainability standards.

Food & Beverages

Freshness and safety are at the heart of Food & Beverage -supply chains. We enable end-to-end visibility with IoT-driven tracking, automated quality checks, and predictive demand planning—ensuring traceability from farm to fork.
